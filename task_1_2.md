Prompt 1 

Original prompt:
Write a social media post for our new shoe brand.

Rewritten Prompt
You are a senior social media strategist working for a premium footwear brand.

Write an Instagram post for a new luxury shoe collection aimed at urban professionals aged 25–40.

Requirements:
- Tone: modern, premium, aspirational
- Keep it under 100 words
- Start with a strong hook
- Include a clear call-to-action
- Add exactly 3 relevant hashtags

Return the output in this format:
Post: <post text>
Hashtags: <hashtag 1>, <hashtag 2>, <hashtag 3>
Explanation

The original prompt was too vague, so I improved it by giving the model a clear role, target audience, platform, and tone. That makes the output more focused and more useful in a real agency setting. I also added length limits and output formatting so the result is easier to review and use directly. The call-to-action and hashtag requirements help make the response closer to something a social media team would actually publish.

Before / After Comparison for Prompt 1
Before Output (using weak prompt)
Check out our new shoe brand. We have stylish and comfortable shoes for everyone. Buy now and upgrade your look.
After Output (using rewritten prompt)
Post: Step into a new standard of style. Our latest luxury shoe collection is designed for professionals who want comfort, confidence, and a polished look in every step. Discover premium craftsmanship made for the pace of modern city life. Explore the collection today.

Hashtags: #LuxuryFootwear, #ModernStyle, #UrbanProfessionals
Short Comparison Note

The weak prompt produced something very generic and flat. The rewritten prompt gave a much stronger result because it included audience, tone, platform, and structure. The final output feels more polished and much closer to real brand content.

Rewrite the ad copy below to make it more engaging, memorable, and emotionally appealing.

Instructions:
- Keep the original meaning the same
- Use stronger wording and more vivid language
- Make the tone feel premium and polished
- Keep the final version under 80 words
- Do not use clichés

Original copy:
{{INPUT_TEXT}}

Return only the rewritten version.
Explanation

The original prompt did not explain what “more creative” actually meant, so the result could go in any direction. I improved it by defining the role, the tone, the goal, and the limits. I also told the model to keep the original meaning, which is important because most ad teams want improvement, not a completely different message. Adding a word limit and style guidance makes the output more controlled and useful.

- Tone of Voice
- Recommended Channels
- Risks or Concerns

Instructions:
- Keep each section short and practical
- Focus on decision-making information
- Avoid repeating unnecessary details

Campaign brief:
{{INPUT_TEXT}}

Return the answer in JSON format.
Explanation

The original prompt was too broad and would likely produce an unstructured summary. I changed it into a task that gives the model a clear role and a specific structure.
